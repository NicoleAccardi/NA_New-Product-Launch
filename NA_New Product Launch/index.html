The Mission Continues

About 1.6 straws per person get used every day. That's over 11 billion straws! Regular plastic straws are not recyclable and do not decompose. This leads to long-term pollution. And, becuase they are light weight, they often blow away and end up in water where they pose a threat to marine life.

Our mission to help reduce waste has led us to make the next high-quality reusable product you need to live your eco-friendly life. Our new Arctic Circle Staws are made of food-grade stainless steel and come with a free brush for cleaning the inside of the straw. All of our straws fit in our Arctic Circle Reusable Waterbottles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Arctic Circle Straws dishwasher safe?

Yes! Although AC Straws are dishwasher safe, we recommend using the brush to clean the inside of the straw before placing in the dishwasher or washing it by hand.

Are Arctic Circle Straws safe for hot beverages?

AC Straws are safe to use with hot beverages. Always use caution when drinking hot beverages.

Can Arctic Circle Straws be placed in the freezer?

AC Straws can absolutely be placed in the freezer.

Can Arctic Circle Sraws be customized?

AC Straws can be customized. We offer laser engravings. Personalized straws are great for everyday use, fundrasing and business promotion. You can add your name, initials, company or school.

Is there a discount for purchasing in bulk?

There is a discount for purchasing in bulk. The discount varies on each order. AC Straws are great party favors, business promotions, and fundraising items especially when customized.
